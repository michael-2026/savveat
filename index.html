Grilled Steak with Asparagus

Cuisine
Western
Prep Time
15 min
Cook Time
20 min
Servings
2 persons
Calories
~520 kcal
Difficulty
⭐⭐⭐

1
Season ribeye steak with pink peppercorns and salt. Drizzle olive oil, rest 10 min.
2
Blanch asparagus in salted boiling water for 2 min. Drain and set aside.
3
Heat cast iron pan until smoking. Grill steak 3–4 min per side for medium rare.
4
Sauté cherry tomatoes and garlic in butter until softened. Season to taste.
5
Plate steak with asparagus and cherry tomatoes. Rest 5 min before serving.
